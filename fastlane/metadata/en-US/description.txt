Set your favorite music or video audio as your alarm sound.

KEY FEATURES

Custom Alarm Sounds
Import audio files or extract audio from videos to use as your alarm. Wake up every morning to the sound you love.

Extract Audio from Videos
Select any part of a video from your Camera Roll and extract the audio. Adjust start and end points while previewing, so you can find the perfect clip.

Audio File Import
Supports MP3, AAC, WAV, and M4A formats. Import directly from the Files app.

Share Sheet Integration
Add audio or video directly from other apps using the share sheet.

Repeat Scheduling
Set alarms to repeat on specific days of the week. Weekdays only, weekends only, or any combination.

SUPPORTED FORMATS
MP3 / AAC / WAV / M4A / Video (MOV, MP4) audio extraction

NOTES
- Requires iOS 26 or later (AlarmKit)
- Alarm sounds of 30 seconds or less are recommended
